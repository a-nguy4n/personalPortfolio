About Me

   Hello! My name is Allison Nguyen! I am currently a student at the University of California, San Diego studying Computer Engineering and minoring in Design. I choose to study Computer Engineering as it provides me the best of both worlds with software and hardware.

   In the future, I aspire to be a product designer of sorts where I can work between software, hardware, and design. I hope to one day develop and design a product that can serve to be a great vitamin for everyday life.

   Beyond my academic journey, I enjoy spending time outdoors and discovering new places. Specifically, I love going around and trying out new coffee shops and bakeries, hiking new trails, or just listening to music on walks.

   A little fun fact about me is that wherever I go or travel to, I collect postcards as a souvenir. I feel like postcards are a small, but meaningful (and inexpensive) way to capture my experiences and snapshots from my travels.
